Services

The BeeSafe project offers many useful features to ensure your child's safety!

Live Tracking

Find out where your child is, anytime, anywhere - on web and mobile!

Virtual Fences

Create areas in which your child has to be at set times!

Instant notifications

Get an instant text on your phone when your child leaves a fence!
